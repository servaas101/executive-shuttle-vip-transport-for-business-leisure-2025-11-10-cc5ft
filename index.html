About Pogiso's Tours

Founded in 2010, Pogiso's Tours began with a simple vision: to revolutionize executive transportation in South Africa. What started as a small operation with just three vehicles has grown into a trusted partner for leading corporations, government agencies, and international travelers. Over the past decade, we've maintained our commitment to excellence while expanding our fleet and service offerings to meet the evolving needs of our discerning clientele. Our journey has been marked by consistent growth, unwavering reliability, and a deep understanding of what it means to provide truly premium service in the transportation industry.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional transportation experiences that exceed expectations and build lasting relationships with our clients. We believe that every journey should be seamless, comfortable, and memorable. Our core values—integrity, professionalism, safety, and customer-centricity—guide every decision we make. We invest in the latest vehicle technology, provide rigorous driver training, and maintain the highest safety standards. At Pogiso's Tours, we don't just transport people; we provide peace of mind, professionalism, and the assurance that you're in capable hands every time you choose us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How far in advance should I book a vehicle?

For regular bookings, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure vehicle availability and optimal service. However, we also offer same-day booking for urgent requirements based on availability. For corporate accounts with recurring bookings, we can arrange standing reservations to guarantee availability for your regular routes. Our 24/7 dispatch team is always ready to accommodate last-minute requests whenever possible.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and cash payments. For corporate clients, we offer flexible billing arrangements with monthly invoicing and customized payment terms. Our corporate account system allows for easy expense tracking and consolidated billing across multiple trips. Payment is typically processed at the time of booking or as per your agreed corporate arrangement.

Are your drivers trained and background-checked?

Absolutely. All our drivers undergo rigorous background checks, including criminal record verification and reference checks. They receive comprehensive training in customer service, vehicle safety, route optimization, and professional etiquette. Our drivers are regularly updated on the latest safety protocols and customer service standards. We maintain strict vetting procedures to ensure only the most qualified and trustworthy professionals represent Pogiso's Tours. Your safety and security are our top priorities.

Can you accommodate special requests or custom itineraries?

Yes, we specialize in customized transportation solutions. Whether you need multi-stop itineraries, specific vehicle types, particular driver preferences, or special amenities, our team works with you to create the perfect solution. We handle everything from airport transfers with city tours to multi-day corporate events and special occasions. Our experienced coordinators will work closely with you to understand your requirements and deliver an experience tailored to your exact specifications.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation options to accommodate your needs. Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the scheduled pickup are free of charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% cancellation fee, while cancellations within 2 hours of pickup may incur a full charge. However, corporate clients can negotiate customized cancellation terms based on their volume and usage patterns. We understand that plans change, and we're committed to working with you to find fair solutions.
